The Best Swamp Soup


Description

The best swamp soup is made with beans, greens, tomatoes with green chiles, and savory Southern smoked sausage. It's a hearty soup, and it's delicious with cornbread!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound smoked sausage, such as Conecuh®, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 jalapenos, seeded and chopped
  • 4 medium gold potatoes, diced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can seasoned turnip greens
  • 1 (14-ounce) can Hoppin' John or seasoned black-eyed peas
  • 1 (10-ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles
  • 1 can navy beans, drained
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons chicken bouillon paste, such as Better Than Bouillon®, or 2 chicken bouillon cubes

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat and add sausage. Cook, stirring often, until sausage is browned and fat has rendered out, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add onion and peppers and cook, stirring constantly until onion softens, about 4 minutes. Add potatoes and cook, stirring often, 2 minutes. Add turnip greens, Hoppin' John, diced tomatoes and green chiles, navy beans, water, and bouillon paste; stir to combine.
  3. Bring mixture to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender, 25 to 30 minutes.
